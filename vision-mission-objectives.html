  Vision , Mission & Objectives

Mission & Vision:

The prime objective of this specialized University will be - to introduce quality education and expand of some yet unexplored nontraditional scientific fields into the Bangladeshi educational curriculum, particularly: Environment Protection Technology, Food Science and Bio-Technology, Agricultural Science & Technology, Bio-Engineering and Medicine (Veterinary medicine and Public Health). These study fields will be categorized in 4-faculties and subdivided under more than 20 departments. Moreover, German experienced Professors will be responsible to maintain the Standard and Quality of Education as well as for the accreditation of Degrees of this University according to European Credit Transfer System (ECTS). The first Vice Chancellor will be Professor Dr. vet. med. Leo Brunnberg, a highly experienced German Professor. Ultimately the University shall serve for building up a reservoir of highly skilled, scientific and technical manpower according to the global needs.


The ultimate vision of IGUB and GUBT are as follows: Implement modernized German Higher Education Concept at GUB Curriculum Mixed Faculty with Teaching & Research options of German Professors/Scientists in Bangladesh and vice versa Access of Bangladeshi Students to German Universities The Students will learn and get the exposure to German work ethics, sincerity and dedication Strengthen the ties between Germany and Bangladeshi
