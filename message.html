  Message From The Vice-Chancellor


German University Bangladesh (GUB) is located at Gazipur where it’s having the glory of being the first Government and UGC approved private University in this region. The University got approval from the Government of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh on 13th October 2013 and launched its academic activities from April 2015. The entrepreneurs of GUB believe that they have social and corporate responsibility that lead them to establish the university with a view to produce a pool of capable and competent graduates equipped with knowledge and skill appropriate to the needs of the 21st century. From the inception, GUB provides an excellent academic environment linked up with societal values, and synchronized by affection and self-respect. It maintains harmony, discipline and strictly follows the academic calendar. We are blessed with knowledgeable, dedicated and distinguished academics as well as scientific and student friendly administrative staffs.



We are committed to preparing our students to work hard and acquire skills helpful for the construction of knowledge societies. GUB aims to be a specialized university with precise and specific purpose of developing skilled manpower in the disciplines that would be mostly needed in the immediate future to address the complex global economic, health and environmental anomalies of the 21st century in this globalized world. In addition to providing quality education, another prime objective of this specialized university is to expand of some yet unexplored nontraditional scientific disciplines into the Bangladesh educational curriculum. GUB aims to ultimately take its place as an international network university in the global competition among universities. The academic support services of this university include the library, computer lab and specialized labs for different disciplines. These services provide support to students to learn more effectively, chart progress, make the most of feedback from tutors and make decisions about future learning needs and career planning. The university has well decorated student hostels, cafeteria and common room facilities. The extra-curricular activities include GUB Debate Club, Computer Club, and Sports Club. However, the long-term strategy of GUB is to be a fully residential private self-sustainable university with important faculties and central institutes offering multiple degree programs across a wide range of subjects.

We strive to uphold and promote the academic excellence, pedagogical sophistication, quality research and social responsibility of this newly established University. I am committed to serve the university with my utmost honesty and sincerity.

It’s my great pleasure to be the part of GUB family and having the leadership to strive for “Noble Way To Build A Better Future”.


Professor Dr. Md. Shams-Ud-Din
Vice-Chancellor
German University Bangladesh
